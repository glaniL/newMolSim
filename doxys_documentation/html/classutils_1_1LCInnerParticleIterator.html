Checks whether the outer particle was already the last Particle in its cell which would mean iterator were now on the dummy end of the list.

Checks the neighoring cells of the outer particle in a predetermined order: right, top left, top, top right [for both dimensional cases], Back bottom left, back bottom, back bottom right, back left, back, back right, back top left, back top and back top right [for the 3-dimensional case]
